# Mileva Marić's Archives

## Overview

**Mileva Marić's Archives** is a static website dedicated to documenting the life, family history, and historical context of Mileva Marić and members of her family.

The project was initiated as a way to **solidify my front-end development skills**, combining technical practice with historical research. Its development was inspired by the integration of knowledge acquired during my **Summer History Course at the University of Cambridge (2022)** and my ongoing studies in **Software Engineering at UniSatc (Brazil)**.

The website functions both as a technical learning project and as a structured digital archive focused on historical preservation and cultural documentation.

---

## Live Website

## Purpose

This project was developed with the following objectives:

* To document historical and biographical information related to Mileva Marić and her family;
* To organize genealogical and contextual historical content in a structured digital format;
* To strengthen front-end development skills through real implementation;
* To explore version control and project maintenance using Git and GitHub;
* To contribute to the dissemination of Serbian historical heritage through digital media.

## Project Structure

The website is organized into individual HTML pages dedicated to specific family members and related historical subjects.

```
mileva-maric-archives/

├── index.html
├── index.css

├── mileva.html
├── mileva.css

├── marija.html
├── marija.css

├── milos-filho.html
├── milospai.html
├── milospai.css

├── zorka.html
├── zorka.css

├── helene.html

├── eduard.html
├── eduard.css

├── imagens/
│   └── (historical photographs and visual materials)

└── README.md
```

Each page focuses on a specific individual within the Marić family, contributing to the archival structure of the project.
(This structure may evolve as new content is added.)
